# FAQcordion Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** 2026-03-03

This Privacy Policy describes how **FAQcordion** (“the App”) collects, uses, and shares information when a merchant installs or uses the App in a Shopify store.

## Information we collect

### Store and merchant information
When you install the App, we receive information from Shopify needed to provide the App, such as:
- Your store domain (e.g., `yourstore.myshopify.com`)
- Basic merchant/store identifiers provided through Shopify’s authentication (OAuth)

### App configuration and content
The App may create and read Shopify **metaobject definitions**, **metaobjects**, and **metafields** in your store to power FAQ display and styling settings. This can include:
- FAQ items (questions and answers)
- Display settings (colors, font sizes, spacing, etc.)

### What we do not collect
FAQcordion is not designed to collect customer personal data (such as customer names, emails, addresses, or payment details). If your store theme or Shopify provides such data in other contexts, the App does not intentionally store it.

## How we use information
We use the information above to:
- Authenticate your store and keep the App working
- Create and manage FAQ content and settings in your Shopify store
- Provide support and troubleshoot issues when you request help

## Sharing information
We do not sell your information. We share information only:
- With Shopify as required to operate within the Shopify platform
- With service providers used to host or run the App (e.g., infrastructure hosting), only as needed to provide the service
- If required by law

## Data retention
We retain store-related App data only as long as needed to provide the App.
- If you uninstall the App, you may remove App-created FAQ metaobjects and metafields from Shopify admin.
- Some data stored in Shopify (metaobjects/metafields) may remain in your store until you delete it.

## Security
We take reasonable steps to protect the information we process. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

## Your choices
You can:
- Edit or delete FAQ items in Shopify Admin (Metaobjects)
- Change display settings in the App
- Uninstall the App at any time
